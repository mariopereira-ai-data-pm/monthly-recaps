💡 Outside-the-Box Plays

Differentiate vs. any LLM-on-dashboards story

📡 Locker-as-Sensor

Thousands of fixed instrumented endpoints = a privately-held urban sensor network. Anonymized foot traffic, parcel volume, weather correlation. Sellable to retailers, urban planners, insurers. No competitor has the footprint.

📊 Carrier Benchmarking

"Your first-attempt rate is bottom quartile vs EU postal companies your size." Carriers will pay for it. Lower technical bar than cross-carrier routing. Monetizable in 2026.

🤝 Founders' Co-pilot

Founders want answers, not dashboards. Make founders the first internal customer of DataBloq's external interface. Forces the semantic layer to be production-grade, fast.

🗑 Kill the Dashboard

Audit dashboard usage logs. Retire 50% of low-usage dashboards in favor of NL queries against the same semantic layer. Gives Prompt Dashboard a captive internal audience before external.

🎭 Synthetic Scenario Factory

Vandalism, fraud, tampering, failed handovers are rare and hard to label. Generative pipeline produces plausible scenarios for training detection models. Defensible capability.

🧬 Locker Digital Twins

Per-asset failure-mode prediction trained on each unit's sensor history. Uniquely possible because Bloq.it owns the hardware. No software-only competitor can build this.

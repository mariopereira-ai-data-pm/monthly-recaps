🎯 The 11 High-Friction Problems · Where Data & AI Pay Back

Quantified pain · scoped solution · this is the spine

Data Problems 5

D1 No Unified View of Network Health Int + Ext
DHL polls our backend every 5 min (~8,640 req/month) because they don't trust our reporting. InPost challenged our SLA figures with incidents we weren't tracking. Partner credibility erosion delays expansion.
Solution: Governed Gold layer + semantic layer. One definition, one answer, everywhere - dashboards, APIs, AI.
D2 Reports Take Disproportionate Effort Internal
10h per partner MBR × 4 partners = 40h/month. At 10 partners, MBR production alone consumes a full FTE doing zero analysis. Scales linearly.
Solution: Gold layer centralises business logic. Streamline MBR Generation: ~10 hours → minutes. Domain self-service via Looker.
D3 75% Parcel Redirection at Saturated Lockers External
At some locations, up to 75% of parcels are redirected because the locker is full at assignment. Higher last-mile cost, worse UX, lower throughput. Carriers won't share data - sensitivity signals the scale.
Solution: Real-time occupancy + smart redirection (AI3). Also a partner data-sharing hook → compounding flywheel.
D4 Partners Place Lockers by Gut Feel External
"Where next?" is answered without data today. Suboptimal placement compounds over the deployment lifecycle. Slow partner expansion = slow Bloq.it revenue. Analysis we could productise goes to third-party consultants.
Solution: Location Intelligence Engine - internal utilisation + external enrichment (demographics, transport, weather, competitors). Premium data product.
D5 No Governed Infrastructure to Deliver Data at Scale Int + Ext
No tenant isolation, no RBAC, no API contract. Without this, DataBloq cannot launch. Every partner integration is bespoke. Security exposure = contractual breach risk.
Solution: Semantic layer + API + tenant isolation + RBAC + caching + multi-format delivery (dashboard, API, CSV, scheduled).

AI Problems 6

AI1 No Predictive Maintenance Capability Int + Ext
NEXT fleet scaling fast. Battery swaps, motor failures, signal drops trigger reactive dispatches. Every truck roll has direct cost. SLA penalties from preventable failures.
Solution: Rule-based thresholds → anomaly detection on battery telemetry. Daily maintenance risk score per bloq. Depends on D1
AI2 Location Scoring is Descriptive, Not Prescriptive External
D4 provides the data. Without ML, the recommendation stays manual. Suboptimal decisions compound over locker lifecycle. Lost expansion velocity per partner.
Solution: ML scoring model on internal + external features. Ranked recommendations with confidence. Depends on D4, D5
AI3 No Demand Forecasting / Occupancy Prediction Int + Ext
D3 gives real-time visibility. Without forecasting, capacity adjustments are always reactive. Peak overload = missed parcels, emergency sites, blind overstaffing.
Solution: Time-series forecasting (Prophet) per location × partner × day. 4/8/24h horizons. Foundation for intelligent routing. Depends on D1, D3
AI4 We Learn About Issues When Partners Complain Int + Ext
InPost cited 5,000 crashed labels and black screens invisible to our SLA reports. Shifts partner relationship from "trusted operator" to "vendor we monitor."
Solution: Statistical anomaly detection on event streams. Classification (hardware vs user vs environment). Alerts with hypothesis before partners notice. Depends on D1
AI5 Manual Partner Insight Generation Won't Scale External
Partners want answers, not dashboards. 40h/month on MBRs today, linear scaling. At 10 partners, MBR production becomes a dedicated FTE doing zero analysis.
Solution: LLM-powered narrative from certified metrics. Cube.dev agentic analytics shares infrastructure with D5. Depends on D1, D2, D5
AI6 Tribal Knowledge Lives Across Tools Internal
Onboarding new joiners takes weeks. Same questions answered repeatedly in Slack. Confluence + Drive + threads are not searchable as one. Hidden cost compounds across every team.
Solution: Bloq.it knowledge layer over Confluence + Slack + Drive. Pattern proven on EVRI - replicates to every account & team. ✓ EVRI prototype validates

Thousands of instrumented endpoints = a private urban sensor network. Anonymized foot traffic, parcel volume, weather correlation. Sellable to retailers, urban planners, insurers.

📊 Carrier Benchmarking

"Your first-attempt rate is bottom quartile vs EU postal companies your size." Carriers will pay. Lower bar than cross-carrier routing. 2027 monetization candidate.

🧬 Locker Digital Twins

Per-asset failure-mode prediction trained on each unit's sensor history. Uniquely possible because Bloq.it owns the hardware. No software-only competitor can build this.

🎭 Synthetic Scenario Factory

Vandalism, fraud, tampering, failed handovers are rare and hard to label. Generative pipeline produces plausible training scenarios. Defensible capability.
